More details about the instructor

Ramalho is a partner, teacher at Turing Workshops and the author of the Fluent Python book. He has led teams for the portals IDG Now, BOL, UOL, AOL Brasil and others, using Python since 1998. As an instructor, he assisted clients such as Citibank, CPqD, Serpro , Presidency of the Republic, Globo.com and Itaú. Speaker at international events such as FISL, PyCon US and OSCON. He helped create the Python Brazil Association and was its president. He is a member of the Python Software Foundation and the founder of Garoa Hacker Clube, the first hackerspace in Brazil.
